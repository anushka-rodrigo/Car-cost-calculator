## üìå Overview
Purchasing a vehicle is one of the largest financial decisions an individual can make. In economies such as Sri Lanka‚Äôs‚Äîwhere fuel price volatility, import taxes, depreciation, and maintenance costs significantly impact ownership‚Äîmaking a **data-driven decision** is essential.

This repository contains **version 1, a console-based application** developed to support an academic research project that constructs a **mathematical model** for analyzing the **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)** of vehicles. The application evaluates vehicle ownership costs over a defined period by systematically incorporating purchase price, depreciation, maintenance, operating, service, and ancillary costs. This is a basic console application.

The tool is designed to convert subjective vehicle purchasing decisions into **objective, quantifiable economic comparisons**.

This version focuses **only on calculations** and validation of the model logic.

## üßÆ Cost Components Considered
The application calculates and aggregates the following cost categories:

- **Initial Purchase Cost**
- **Depreciation**
  - Reducing balance depreciation method
- **Maintenance Costs**
  - Tyres, brake pads, fluids, and wear-based replacements
- **Operating Costs**
  - Fuel consumption
  - Insurance
- **Service Costs**
  - Periodic servicing
  - Emissions testing
- **Ancillary Costs**
  - Vehicle washing
  - Miscellaneous expenses (percentage-based)
- **Residual Value**
  - Estimated resale value after ownership period

All vehicles are evaluated under the **same assumptions** to ensure fairness and objectivity.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Assumptions Used in Calculations
- Fuel prices remain constant over the 3-year period
- All wear components are brand new at purchase
- Monthly travel distance is consistent
- Lease payments are excluded during ownership period
- Vehicles are purchased with the intent to resell
- Maximum ownership lifespan capped at 10 years

These assumptions are aligned with the academic research and can be modified in future versions.
